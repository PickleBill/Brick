Primary objective: land a GTM/partnerships seat at a frontier AI lab or top AI startup (the forward-deployed hybrid). Run a fractional/advisory track in parallel for cash + reach.

## 30 days — set the trap, work the warmest threads
- **Make the site "send-ready":** custom domain (billbricker.com), portrait, one named reference, 60–90s Loom. (These 4 are the gating P0s — see OPEN-QUESTIONS.)

- **Stand up the fractional track:** join 1–2 fractional platforms (GTM 80/20, Fractional Jobs) for parallel cash/reach.

## 60 days — convert conversations → loops
- **Run the give-first engine:** lead 15+ relationships with "who can I introduce you to?"; convert replies into referrals (referrals ~7x offer rate).
- **Tailor a "why this lab" artifact** per top target (a 1-page partnership thesis) — labs grade mission-fit + a written memo; this doubles as interview prep.
- **Interview prep:** rehearse the Anthropic-style written memo + customer-conversation simulation (interview-loops.md); build a POV on Constitutional AI/RSP.
- **Ship the AI-ops proof:** wire the Design→Code watcher (AI-OPS) — a live automation you can show.
- Target: 3–5 active processes.

## 90 days — close
- **Drive 2–3 processes to onsite/final**; use the deal sheet + Loom to keep momentum.
- **Negotiate from the comp research** (gtm-hunt-2026.md): anchor senior builder-seller at $450K–$785K+, mostly equity; understand the equity/illiquidity tradeoff.
- **Decide the fork** (OPEN-Q #14): if no lab seat lands by ~day 90, lean into a venture-studio/FIR path (PSL/AI2/SPC) or scale the fractional book.
