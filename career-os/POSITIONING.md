## Recommendation: lead with A, hold B–D as facets

**Why A.** The 2026 research is unambiguous: the **builder+seller "forward-deployed" hybrid is the most-wanted, most-undersupplied profile in AI GTM** (FDE postings +1,165% YoY; "the shift from 'here's what I'd do' to 'here's what I built' is the biggest unlock"). That is *exactly* Bill, and it commands the highest comp ceiling ($450K–$1M+). It also uniquely fits the "the artifact proves the claim" thesis the site is built on. Options B–D are real, but each narrows the aperture or lowers the ceiling; treat them as **facets reachable from the same site**, not separate identities:
- The site leads with **A**.
- **C** (AI-native builder) is the *proof layer* under A — the builds/terminal/pipeline are how A is made falsifiable.
- **B** (fractional) is a near-term cash-flow path and a secondary CTA, not the headline.
- **D** (Courtana) is **proof of building**, not the pitch — one strong case study, not the spine.

**One identity, four doors.** A recruiter, a founder, a fractional client, and a sports-tech partner should each find their path from one site without four sites (the Julian Shapiro / Elena Verna pattern).
