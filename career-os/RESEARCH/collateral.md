# Job-Search Collateral for Senior GTM/Partnerships at AI Labs (2026)

What beats a traditional resume. Confidence inline.

## The hierarchy (what to invest in)
1. **Portfolio website on a custom domain** — 56% of hiring managers are more impressed by a personal site than any other branding tool, yet only ~7% of seekers have one. Companies increasingly ask for portfolio links before the resume. [strong] recruitingdaily.com, name.com — *Bill already has this; the gap is the custom domain.*
2. **Portfolio projects / case studies / deal log** — "A resume tells people what you sold. A portfolio shows how you sell." For BD/sales: case studies (context→problem→strategy→execution→results→learnings) + messaging assets + process docs. [strong] huntyourtribe.com
3. **Loom/video pitch** — video messaging +300% engagement vs written; 5-min screens reveal working style; better 90-day performance in screened cohorts. Keep 5–10 min, scripted-not-read, focus on *approach* not just deliverables. [strong] loom.com, atlassian.com
4. **Deal sheet / brag doc** — company, deal size, date, your role, results (revenue/partnerships/cost), challenges resolved, learnings. Narrative or table. Underutilized by senior BD candidates. Tailor to target company's values. [moderate] wallstreetmojo.com, cnbc.com
5. **Two-page resume + portfolio link** — recruiters prefer 2-page for senior/VP roles by 2.3x; one-pager at VP level signals "haven't done much." 3–5 results-with-numbers bullets/role + strong opening summary. Anchor to the portfolio (removes space limits). [strong] atscvchecker.pro, rezi.ai
6. **One-pager (GTM philosophy + key partnerships + outcomes)** — low-friction, "moved not read," forwarded/saved. Avoid attaching full decks to cold outreach ("desperation"). [moderate] fundingblueprint.io

## What frontier labs specifically respond to
- **Public work > credentials.** Anthropic prioritizes independent research, blog posts, open-source as top resume signals; ~50% of technical staff lack PhDs — "capability evidence, not degrees, are the gatekeepers." [strong] letsdatascience.com
- For GTM: 3–5 artifacts with **measured outcomes** ("2.8x reply rate," pipeline generated); live execution / "vibe coding" in interviews; titles/years matter less. [strong] superinterviews.substack.com
- **Relationships convert capability into offers.** OpenAI gets 400K+ applications/yr; referred candidates ~7x more likely to get offers. 6–12 month relationship path (visible work → engage research → conferences → 2–3 internal relationships → referral). [strong]

## Custom domain + email
- Custom domain (yourname.com) aids SEO/discoverability + signals intentionality; pair with custom email for outbound (61% of recruiters reject unprofessional addresses; 75% judge credibility by domain). Gmail is *fine* for passive applying; the edge is for outbound. Ideal domain 3–14 chars, .com/.io/.bio. [moderate] name.com, neo.space
