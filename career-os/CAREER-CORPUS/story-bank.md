# Story Bank

Reusable narrative beats for the site, interviews, outreach, and the terminal. Drawn from Brick `content/stories/*` + the résumé + the interview material. Publicity per Bill.

## 1. The Bridge (Dreamship → Courtana, through The Long Walk) — public
September 2023: cancer diagnosis. Bill stepped back from Dreamship CEO to Board Chair **with the Google deal already in flight** — and the company stayed profitable every year since. He did chemo through 2025 and **built anyway**: Courtana, cameras on pickleball courts — passion, product, and profit in one place. Now Courtana runs on the side while he walks toward the next thing: something AI-native, more human, faster loops. *"That walk is this site."*
- **Use for:** the human, differentiating arc; the "why now / why AI" pivot. Lead with operate-through-anything, not illness.
- **Scene:** negotiated the Microsoft/Bing-ChatGPT partnership from Duke hospital, mid-chemo.

## 2. The Google Deal (the room) — public; economics blur
See deal-case-studies.md #1. Narrative hook: a no-name startup walks Google through 5–6 internal teams and leaves with a first-of-its-kind partnership — by making every party win. Then Stripe, PayPal, Payoneer, Meta line up. *"There's something about creating multi-party BD deals where the incentives are aligned."*

## 3. The Builder Turn (career seller → ships AI himself) — public
Twenty years selling frontier-ish tech — IBM/Netezza big data, WibiData, then founder-led sales at Dreamship — then Bill stopped only *selling* the technology and started *building* it: 40+ apps across 31 repos, an 11-agent simulator, a sports-CV pipeline, and a résumé that runs on its own AI. *The seller learned to ship.* This is the spine of the "forward-deployed" positioning.

## 4. The Networking Manifesto ("Who can I introduce you to?") — public
Bill's give-first operating philosophy: lead relationships by offering introductions, not asking for favors. Validated by the research (Adam Grant / Ferrazzi reciprocity). Still operative — and the right *first move* in the job hunt (offer intros to the people you want to reach).

## 5. Origin texture — publicity ⚠️
DJ Billygoat; Northwestern Mutual top-10 selling; the College of NJ rugby captaincy; IBM/Netezza war stories. ⚠️ Tell or keep as easter eggs? (OPEN-Q #12). One specific true detail in the open (the 🥒 / "fka DJ Billygoat" eyebrow) is the anti-slop personality signal — keep at least that.
